Explore Biryani Varieties

Hyderabadi Biryani

Known for its strong spices and aromatic saffron, Hyderabadi biryani is cooked in a traditional dum method with basmati rice and marinated meat. It's rich, flavorful, and iconic across India.

Lucknowi (Awadhi) Biryani

A milder, fragrant version of biryani made with ghee, rose water, and slow-cooked meat. It's layered delicately and famous for its royal taste from the Nawabi kitchens of Lucknow.

Chicken Biryani

A popular version where tender chicken is layered with spiced rice and herbs. It’s commonly found in home kitchens and restaurants alike, loved by all ages.

Mutton Biryani

A rich and hearty dish made with slow-cooked mutton that falls off the bone, blended with aromatic rice and spices. It's a festival favorite.

Veg Biryani

For vegetarian lovers, this biryani includes mixed vegetables, paneer, and nuts, layered with masala rice. Despite no meat, it's full of flavor and often paired with raita.

Egg Biryani

A quick and protein-rich version of biryani where boiled or fried eggs are combined with spiced rice, perfect for a simple yet delicious meal.
